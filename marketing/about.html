PEOPLE.

Zoë’s Café is a locally owned, non-profit coffee house and event center in the heart of Greeley’s Downtown. We serve ethically and relationally sourced coffee and loose leaf tea from The Tea Spot. We prepare salads, sandwiches, and wraps fresh daily to stock the cooler at Zoë’s.

Centered around beauty and justice, we host art displays, and community and private events, among other things; we are passionate about our community, and we seek to engage it on every level we can!
