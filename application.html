How to Play

This is a 2D platformer game where you control a cat on a quest to collect coins while avoiding deadly red spikes!

Use the arrow keys and spacebar to navigate the level. Collect all the coins to win — but don’t touch the spikes or you'll have to start over!

  • ⬅️ ➡️ Arrow keys — Move left/right
  • Spacebar — Jump
  • Collect all coins to beat the level
  • Avoid red spikes or you’ll die and restart
