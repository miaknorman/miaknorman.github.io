How to Play

This is a fun platformer game where you help Scratch Cat collect coins and avoid deadly spikes!

If you touch a spike or fall off the platform, you'll see a Game Over screen. Make it to the end and you'll win!

  • Move: Use the arrow keys to go left or right
  • Jump: Press the spacebar to jump
  • Goal: Avoid all the spikes and reach the finish
