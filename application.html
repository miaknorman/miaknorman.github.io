How to Play

This game is a fun dodge-the-asteroids challenge created on Scratch. You control a spaceship that must avoid crashing into falling space debris.

Use the arrow keys to move your ship side to side. The longer you survive, the higher your score!

  • Use ← and → arrow keys to steer
  • Avoid the asteroids
  • Try to beat your personal best score!
